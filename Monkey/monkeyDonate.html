Support the Monkey's

There are many of monkeys around the world that need help, and one way to help them is to donate.  When donating to these animals, it can help bigger organisations create reserves where the monkeys can stay in the wild, but still be in a safe environment where there are no threats to their lives.  Also, there is an message box, where you can give ideas and/or thanks to the organisation, or any questions that haven't been answered from the website.
