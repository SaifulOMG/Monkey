Special Events

Below are multiple events that will be happening in the near future, we ask you to attend these events to help us support the monkeys

Market shopping will be coming soon to be able to raise money for the monkeys, and donations will be accepted there.

We will have a trip that's going to Monkey World which is an ape rescue centre.  It will be an educational trip that helps show why we need to protect apes, and show how many are in danger currently.

One of our charity workers will be travelling to schools, to explain why we need to help protect these monkeys, and why we must raise awareness.

There will be a festival happening in attempt to raise money and awareness for the monkeys.  All proceeds made during the festival will be given to help protect the monkeys.
