About Us

We are an organisation that is working to try to stop illegal and barbaric trade of monkeys, that is unfortunately occurring all over the world.  These monkeys deserve a life and we will work to make sure they can stay in their wild habitat safely.  The only reason we would ever take them out of their natural habitat is to help them and once they have fully recovered we return them home.

Vision

Monkeys around the world are being removed from their natural habitats for the use of illegal pet trading or for entertainment. Our vision is that all monkeys can someday be safe from poaching, and will not have issues with endangerment.
